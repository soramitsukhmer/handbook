1. Google Suite (with 2FA)
2. Slack (with 2FA)
3. Jira and Confluence (with 2FA)

**Designers**
1. Figma (with 2FA)

**Developers & Engineers**
1. GitHub (with 2FA)
2. Get access to infrastructure for delivery team
